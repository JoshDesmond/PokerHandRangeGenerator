Poker Range Generator

Instructions

This tool allows you to generate your pre-flop ranges for Texas Hold'em. You can set the percentage of hands you want to include in your early position range, set a slightly tigher range of hands to play from late position range, and then indicate the smallest percentage of hands you would 3-bet with.
You can select which hand ranking type you want to compare your hand against. Vs. Tight 9 ranks hands by the probability they win against nine opponents who fold weak hands. Vs. Random 1 ranks hands by the probability they win against a single random hand. And Random 9 ranks hands by the probability they win against nine other random hands.

Range Input:

3 Bet Range: top %

Early Range: top %

Late Range: top %

Hand Ranking Type:

vs Tight 9 vs Random 1 vs Random 9
